# Blog Post Template

## Structure

### Title
- Clear, specific, and compelling
- Include the primary keyword naturally
- Aim for 50-60 characters

### Introduction (10-15% of total length)
- Hook: open with a surprising fact, question, or relatable problem
- Context: briefly explain why this topic matters
- Thesis: state what the reader will learn or gain

### Body sections (70-80% of total length)
- 3-5 main sections, each with a descriptive heading
- Each section covers one key point
- Include examples, data, or anecdotes to support claims
- Use subheadings for sections over 300 words

### Conclusion (10-15% of total length)
- Summarize key takeaways (2-3 bullet points)
- Restate the value proposition
- Call to action: what should the reader do next?

## Formatting best practices

- Paragraphs: 2-4 sentences max
- Use bullet lists for 3+ related items
- Bold key terms on first use
- Include at least one example per major section
- Break up long sections with subheadings
